Feed Me


At Feed Me you get to eat out with good conscience both on an ordinary, informal Tuesday and on a festive Saturday. A visit at our restaurant is not associated with big economic challenges. However you get an overload of delicious food and a cozy atmosphere.

About:

Let us challenge your taste buds in our experimental and unconventional kitchen. In our kitchen, the sky’s the limit – we push the boundaries, set to decide what’s possible – and legal! At Feed Me we are not afraid to play with new taste combinations or raw materials.

Our menu is created by the chef, and it is simple and understandable. The chef has carefully selected his own favorite dishes and with new raw materials he has lifted them to a whole new level. A level that kicks a**! Look forward to biting into delicious Danish classics – you have never had them like this before!

We went all the way to give you a hell of a night! Step into the Feed Me universe and explore the symphony of amazing raw materials, sassy serving, neon signs and playful culinary dishes.
